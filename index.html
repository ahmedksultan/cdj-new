Cornell Data Journal is an online investigative journalism publication featuring data visualization, data communication, and interdisciplinary research pieces.

Data journalism is an emerging subfield of journalism, and often combines data, visual, and narrative reporting into digital stories.
Examples include isometric illustrations, statistical explorations, and interactive essays.

We welcome students of all backgrounds and disciplines to come create with us.
Whether you've worked with data before or not, anyone is welcome to contribute and publish with us.
